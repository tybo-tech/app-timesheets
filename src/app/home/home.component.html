To see to ensure that the core principles of good care are fundamental to NMC Code of conduct : standards of conduct, performance and ethics for nurses and midwives (2008) states that a nurse has a duty of care at all times and people must be able to trust them with their lives and health. To justify that trust, they must: • Make the care or people their first concern, treating them as individuals and respecting their dignity. • Work with other to protect and promote the health and wellbeing of those in their care • Provide a high standard of practice and care at all times • Be open and honest, act with integrity and uphold the reputation of their profession
